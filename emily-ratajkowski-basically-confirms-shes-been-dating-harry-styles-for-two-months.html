Emily Ratajkowski is Reportedly Dating Harry Styles

It seems like Hollywood has a new power couple in the making as rumors are circulating that Ratajkowski Ratajkowski and Styles are dating. Fans have been eagerly following their every move since Ratajkowski confirmed their relationship last month. But, what do we know about their romance so far?
