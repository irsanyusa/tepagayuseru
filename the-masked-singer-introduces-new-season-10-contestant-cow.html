The Masked Singer has introduced a new contestant for Season 10, named Cow. The show recently revealed Cow’s costume on social media ahead of the official season premiere later this month. Fans are already talking about who might be under the mask.

The Masked Singer Reveals Season 10 Cow Costume

Over the weekend, The Masked Singer posted a video revealing Cow’s costume. It starts with the celebrity standing outside in their “DON’T TALK TO ME” hoodie, with their face covered. Then the post shows the costume.

Cow stands against a farm background, with a bottle of milk on the ground nearby. They’re wearing a blue gingham dress with a red-accented apron. They also have matching red eyeglasses and red lips. Cow also has short black hair with a blue headband.

The post teases that Cow is a “‘MOO-SICALLY’ gifted singer.” Fans are already speculating about their identity, with guesses on Instagram including RuPaul, Adam Lambert, Ariana Grande, Martha Stewart, and Rebel Wilson. We can’t wait to learn more clues.

Season 10 Kicked Off This Week

On Sunday, The Masked Singer kicked off Season 10 with a special episode ahead of the official season premiere on Wednesday, September 27, on FOX. During the episode, a character called Anonymouse took the stage. At the end of the night, they were revealed to be singer Demi Lovato.

Sunday’s kickoff episode also featured duets from past contestants on the show, including Rumer Willis, Michelle Williams, Bow Wow, and Joey Fatone. Panelist Nicole Scherzinger even took the stage with two of her crushes, Victor Oladipo and Barry Zito.

The show has introduced us to quite a few Season 10 contestants already, including Donut, Hawk, S’More, Hibiscus, Diver, Gazelle, Royal Hen, and Husky. Are you excited for the new season to start? Let us know in the comments below.
