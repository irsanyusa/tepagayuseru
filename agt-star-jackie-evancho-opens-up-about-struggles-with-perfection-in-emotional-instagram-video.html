'AGT' Star Jackie Evancho Opens Up About Struggles With Perfection in Emotional Instagram Video

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

Photo by Raymond Hall/GC Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Renowned singer Jackie Evancho recently surprised her followers on Instagram with a deeply personal video where she addressed the pressures she faced growing up in the entertainment industry and her ongoing battle with the need for perfection. In the heartfelt post, Evancho shared her journey toward self-acceptance and highlighted the importance of confronting societal expectations.

Jackie Evancho Opens Up About Struggles With Perfection

In the emotional video, Evancho explained that she was taking a different approach this time, speaking to her audience in a more personal and authentic manner. She expressed how challenging it is to talk about something deeply ingrained in her, even during therapy sessions. The subject she addressed is the societal pressure to attain perfection, a struggle she believes many people face.

Growing up in the entertainment industry presented Evancho with its own set of challenges. While acknowledging the wonderful aspects of her career, she revealed that the pressure to be perfect accompanied her journey.

As Evancho matured, she began to question the idea of perfection and the need for everyone’s approval. She realized that striving for an unattainable standard was futile. Evancho expressed that she no longer believes she needs to be perfect in order to be seen or heard.

Evancho’s willingness to share this intimate aspect of her life serves as a reminder of the importance of mental health awareness and the significance of seeking support and understanding in navigating such challenges. Her vulnerability in discussing her disorders opens up a conversation about self-care and emphasizes the importance of prioritizing one’s mental and emotional well-being.

She Opened Up About an Eating Disorder

Additionally, in her Instagram video, Evancho openly acknowledged that her struggles with perfection have contributed to her disordered eating and anxieties. By addressing these disorders, she shed light on the toll that striving for an unattainable ideal can have on one’s well-being.

Evancho then announced her decision to share a song she wrote last year. She described the song as imperfect, recorded late at night in a hotel room using a simple setup. As a vocalist, she confessed being particularly hard on herself when it comes to her vocals. However, she acknowledged that her worth extends beyond her voice, and she no longer seeks validation solely through her singing abilities.

The song Evancho shared represents her defiance against the pressures of perfection. It is a declaration to those who have contributed to her feelings of inadequacy, telling them to leave her life. She emphasized that she no longer wishes to adhere to their opinions and critiques. This confrontational aspect of her video showcases her determination to confront her own demons and move forward.
