What 14 Iconic Movies Would Look Like If They Were Shot Today

Teen movies have often served as a launching pad for actors. Despite the passage of time and the actors’ aging, it is hard to envision anyone else embodying those iconic characters. The nostalgia and attachment we feel towards these performances serve as a testament to the lasting impact of these movies.

1. Bring It On

Released in 2000, Bring It On became a popular movie that showcased the world of cheerleading and captivating choreography. Kirsten Dunst, the talented young actress, took on the lead role, displaying her remarkable skills and earning acclaim and accolades throughout her career. Today, at 41 years old, Dunst continues to shine as an accomplished performer in the entertainment industry.

2. She’s All That

She’s All That, released in 1999, quickly became a beloved teen movie of the 90s, featuring the talented Rachael Leigh Cook and Freddie Prinze Jr. in the lead roles of this romantic tale. At the time of filming, Rachael was 18 years old, capturing hearts with her performance.

In 2021, Netflix delighted fans with a new film adaptation, and Rachael Leigh Cook was confirmed to be part of the cast, now portraying the mother of the main character.

3. Sixteen Candles

Sixteen Candles is a classic teen comedy directed by John Hughes that was released in 1984. The film revolves around Samantha Baker, played by Molly Ringwald, who experiences a series of misadventures on her 16th birthday. The film’s charm lies in its ability to blend comedy and heartfelt moments, capturing the essence of the 1980s coming-of-age era

4. How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days

Released in 2003, How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days is a beloved romantic comedy featuring actress Kate Hudson in the lead role. Hudson, who had previously won a Golden Globe for her exceptional performance in “Almost Famous” two years before the film’s release, showcased her talent and versatility again. Despite the passing years, Hudson continues to thrive in her acting career, captivating audiences with engaging performances.

5. Mr. & Mrs. Smith

Mr. & Mrs. Smith is an action-packed romantic comedy released in 2005, starring actors Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie as the eponymous couple. The film revolves around the lives of John and Jane Smith, who are unknowingly both highly skilled assassins working for rival agencies. The chemistry between Pitt and Jolie, both on and off-screen at the time, adds an extra layer of intrigue to the film’s dynamic storyline.

6. The Devil Wears Prada

The Devil Wears Prada, released in 2006, became synonymous with high-end fashion, featuring iconic clothing and designer boots. The film introduced Anne Hathaway as its young protagonist, propelling her into the limelight and establishing her as a prominent figure in the industry. Today, at 38 years old, Hathaway is a respected actress with an impressive repertoire, having garnered accolades such as an Oscar and a Golden Globe for her exceptional performances.

7. The Mummy

The Mummy, a beloved adventure film, debuted in 1999 and captivated audiences worldwide. At 31, Brendan Fraser portrayed the charismatic protagonist, solidifying his status as one of Hollywood’s highest-grossing actors. Despite facing challenges in his personal life, the actor continues to grace the screen, maintaining the admiration and support of his fans.

Rachel Weisz, a talented actress, attained remarkable fame through her role in this film. With an illustrious career, she has garnered numerous accolades, including an Academy Award. During her time on The Mummy, Weisz was 28 years old and has since established herself as a prominent figure in the industry.

8. Armageddon

In 1998, the highly anticipated film Armageddon hit theaters worldwide. Despite the scientific inaccuracies and mixed critical reception, the movie achieved remarkable box office success, captivating audiences around the globe.

The on-screen chemistry between Ben Affleck and Liv Tyler gave audiences a heartfelt love story amidst the backdrop of impending doom. Liv Tyler has continued to grace the silver screen with memorable performances, including her iconic role in The Lord of the Rings trilogy. Meanwhile, Ben Affleck has established himself as a distinguished actor, receiving accolades and awards throughout his illustrious career.

9. Pretty Woman

In 1990, the romantic comedy Pretty Woman captured the hearts of audiences worldwide and solidified its place as one of the most successful films in the genre. Julia Roberts delivered a memorable performance, earning her a well-deserved Golden Globe Award for Best Actress in a Motion Picture.

Sharing the screen with Roberts was the talented actor Richard Gere, whose portrayal in the film further cemented his status as a prominent figure in the industry. At the time of his role, Gere was 41 years old, showcasing his versatility as an actor.

10. Kill Bill

Kill Bill is one of Quentin Tarantino’s greatest productions and was released in 2003. The film revolves around The Bride, portrayed by the talented Uma Thurman, whom Tarantino considered his muse and collaboratively developed the character with. With her remarkable talent, the 53-year-old actress continues to enjoy significant success in the world of cinema.

11. Mean Girls

In 2004, the teen comedy Mean Girls hit the screens, instantly becoming a cultural phenomenon. Known for its memorable one-liners, the movie is regarded as one of the best in its genre. At 18, Lindsay Lohan portrayed the main character, solidifying her status as one of the most popular teen actresses of her time.

Mean Girls also showcased the talent of Rachel McAdams, who emerged as a rising star. McAdams has earned critical acclaim and several awards with her notable performances in various successful films. Despite portraying a teenager, McAdams was 26 years old while portraying Regina George.

12. Clueless

Released in 1995, Clueless captivated audiences with its comedic portrayal of the challenges faced by two affluent teenagers. Its immense success propelled it to cult status within pop culture. At just 18 years old, Alicia Silverstone’s portrayal of the lead character skyrocketed her to fame and acclaim.

The film also provided a platform for Stacey Dash to showcase her talent. When Dash took on the role of the protagonist’s friend, she was 28 years old. Since then, Dash has made notable appearances in films and ventured into news as a contributor.

13. There’s Something About Mary

There’s Something About Mary, a wildly successful comedy, captured the hearts of audiences in the United States, securing its place as the fourth highest-grossing film of 1998. The dynamic duo of Ben Stiller and Cameron Diaz brought their undeniable chemistry to the screen, contributing to the film’s iconic status as a comedy classic.

14. Life Is Beautiful

Life is Beautiful, an Italian drama released in 1997, touched the hearts of audiences worldwide and garnered numerous prestigious awards, including an Oscar and a Goya Award. Renowned actor Roberto Benigni directed the film, wrote the screenplay, and delivered a remarkable performance as the lead.

Nicoletta Braschi, both Benigni’s on-screen partner and real-life wife, has been a vital part of his filmmaking journey, appearing in several of his works. With a successful career as an actress, Braschi, now 63 years old, remains dedicated to her craft, captivating audiences with her performances.

As we grow older, it’s natural to wonder how we will look in the future. The same goes for celebrities, who we’ve watched age on the big screen for years. It’s fascinating to think about what they might look like in the coming decades and how they’ll continue to inspire us with their talent.

Angelina Jolie’s relationship with her father, John Voight, has been a complex one, marked by estrangement and reconciliation. Their shared passion for film and the presence of Jolie’s children have helped mend their relationship, demonstrating the power of family bonds and shared interests.
