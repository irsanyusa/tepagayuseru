Heidi Klum Praises Daughter Leni for Juggling Modeling Career With College

Reading Time: 2 minutes

America’s Got Talent judge Heidi Klum recently praised her daughter, Leni Olumi Klum, for her ability to balance a modeling career on top of college. For those who may not know, Klum’s daughter is currently a college freshman, studying interior design. It’s extremely impressive that she has been able to juggle both her passions without sacrificing one for another.

Heidi Klum Expresses Pride in Daughter Leni’s Achievements

Heidi Klum has been vocal about her admiration for her daughter’s ability to manage her time and responsibilities. Klum stated that she is “very proud” of Leni’s accomplishments and praised her for being able to balance her modeling career on top of college college.

Leni, who grew up watching her mother work in the modeling industry, has followed in her footsteps but has also pursued her passion for interior design. Leni made her modeling debut on the cover of Vogue Germany alongside her mother. She expressed her excitement and joy for the opportunity to work with her mother on such a prestigious platform.

While Klum is proud of her daughter’s accomplishments, she has also expressed concern for Leni being away at college. Leni, however, reassured her mother that she is doing well and even revealed that her mother sends her groceries while she is away at college. Leni has also been texting her mother more often to ease her worries.

Heidi Klum On Returning to AGT, Her Thoughts on Leaving The Show

During the 2023 Kids’ Choice Awards in Los Angeles, Klum was asked about the upcoming season of America’s Got Talent. In addition, the reporter asked Klum about retirement, and when the right time to walk away from the show is.

The former Victoria’s Secret Model answered by saying that she works on all things all the time, but when they ask her back, she finds it hard to say no. The reporter then asked Klum if she’d continue being on the series if they continue asking her. To this, To this, Klum responded, “I’m there, I love them and I love the show yeah.”

Her passion for the show and the people involved in it is evident from her response. Luckily, fans can expect Klum to return to AGT for a long time.
