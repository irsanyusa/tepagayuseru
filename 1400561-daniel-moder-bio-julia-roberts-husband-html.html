Daniel Moder bio: what is known about Julia Roberts husband?

Who is Julia Roberts husband? His name is Daniel Moder, and just like his famous wife, he is also succeeding in the film industry. Moder is known for his work on Deja Vu (2006), Crimson Tide (1995) and The Forgotten (2004).

Daniel Moder's biography

Daniel Moder was born on January 31, 1969, in Los Angeles, California, to Mike Moder and Patricia Ann-Waltz. His parents worked in the entertainment industry as small-time film producers.

Danny has four siblings: John, Jyl, Jane and Debbie.

Education

The cinematographer graduated from Saint Monica High School in 1987. He then proceeded to the University of Colorado - Boulder and graduated from the university in 1992 with a Psychology degree.

Career

Danny first worked as a production assistant for the film Crimson Tide (1995). He was then hired as a camera loader for the movies The Fan (1996) and The Brave (1997).

The cinematographer was a second assistant camera person on the movie Enemy of the State (1998) and an additional assistant cameraperson for the movie The Big Tease (1999).

He also worked as a first/second assistant cameraperson for the films Tuesday with Morrie (1999), Jerks (2000), and Lucky Numbers (2000).

Relationship with Julia Roberts

Who is Julia Roberts married to? She is married to Daniel Moder. Roberts and Danny first met in 2000. At the time, Danny was working as a cameraman on the set of The Mexican, while Julia was starring opposite Brad Pitt in the 2001 film.

At the time, neither of them was single - Danny was married to Vera Steimberg, and Julia was dating actor Benjamin Pratt. They later split with their respective partners and started dating.

The couple got married on July 4, 2002, on a ranch in Taos, New Mexico. Roberts wore a pale pink cotton halter dress embroidered with pearls and antique beads and hand-painted with flowers. Meanwhile, Moder donned a red ruffled shirt and tan pants for the occasion.
