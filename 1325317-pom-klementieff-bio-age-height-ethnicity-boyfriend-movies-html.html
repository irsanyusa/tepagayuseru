The early life of Pom

Klementieff was born in Quebec City, Canada, where her parents were living at that time. Her father was French-Russian and he worked with the government of France as a consul, while her mother is Korean. So, is Pom Klementieff Korean? Pom Klementieff ethnicity is French.

Unfortunately, when she was only five years old, she lost her father to cancer. Because her mother had schizophrenia, taking care of Pom and her siblings became challenging. Consequently, her paternal uncle and aunt had to raise her.

When she clocked 18 years, her uncle died, and when she was 25, she lost her older brother to suicide.

For her education, Klementieff attended law school briefly after the death of her uncle to please her aunt even though the career did not appeal to her. Later on, she dropped the course to pursue her passion. At the age of 19 years, she began acting.
