Demi Lovato, much like the rest of us, is a total fangirl when it comes to Law & Order: SVU. The singer will have one of her songs featured on the show tonight, and fans can hardly contain their excitement. She even got a special shoutout from the show’s leading lady.

Demi Lovato’s Song Will Be on Tonight’s ‘Law & Order: SVU‘

Law & Order: SVU star Mariska Hargitay tweeted out the hashtag “#DemiSongOnSVUConfirmed” on April 21. The tweet received over 6,000 likes. The comments section blew up with speculation about which song it is.

Earlier today, the official Law & Order Twitter account tweeted out “Is *ANYONE* watching tonight’s #SVU?”

“Anyone” is the title of the hit single off of Lovato’s recent album Dancing with the Devil…the Art of Starting Over. She performed the song live at the  62nd Annual Grammy Awards in 2020. This marked her first live performance since her near-fatal drug overdose in 2018. The song was recorded just days before her overdose.

Once it was publicized that Lovato’s song would appear on Law & Order: SVU, the singer turned into a total fangirl.

“OMFG NOT…OLIVIA F*CKING BENSON HERSELF TWEETING ME N MY SONG ON SVU??!!!!” she wrote on her Instagram story.

Hargitay has portrayed the breakout role of Olivia Benson on the show since 1999. She has also directed several episodes throughout the series and been nominated for countless awards. Her longtime Law & Order: SVU co-star Ice-T appeared on the very first season of The Masked Dancer as The Disco Ball last year.

Fans are Buzzing After Hearing this News

Fans of Lovato and Law & Order: SVU are certainly going to have their worlds collide later tonight in this epic crossover.

The next step is to get Lovato to guest star on the show. So many famous faces have made cameos on the show including Hilary Duff, Miranda Lambert, and Ludacris. The storylines get more and more captivating each season.

Lovato previously had a guest spot on the sixth season of Grey’s Anatomy and fans think it is finally time that she join Law & Order: SVU.

While all signs are pointing toward “Anyone” being the song that the producers decide to feature on the episode, the show is full of curveballs. Twitter is still full of speculation that perhaps a new song will debut on the show.

Regardless of which of Lovato’s hits will be played on the show, the Lovatics, her loyal fans, will all be tuning in to tonight’s episode called “In the Year We All Fell Down.”

The description of the episode sounds like it will be emotional and full of drama.

“Benson gets held hostage while visiting a nearby restaurant,” the description for the episode says. “Meanwhile, Rollins has to contend with the recent hospitalization of her father.”
