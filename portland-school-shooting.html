Portland School Shooting: 3 Injured at Rosemary Anderson High School

Updated: December 12, 2014 4:49 PM [ET] | Originally published: December 12, 2014 4:27 PM EST;

Three people were injured Friday when a shooter opened fire at a high school in Portland, Ore., police said.

The shooter was still at large Friday afternoon, the Associated Press reports. It took place at Rosemary Anderson High School, and the three victims were all conscious. Rosemary Anderson is an alternative high school for students who had previously dropped out, homeless teens, and at-risk students. It enrolls 190 students annually.

A school shooting last occurred in Portland in June when a freshman killed another student and then himself.
