Nicole Scherzinger Shares Hilarious Workout Video with Flula Borg

For months, The Masked Singer panelist Nicole Scherzinger has been sharing her intense workout routine. Recently, the former Pussycat Dolls member posted a funny workout video with German comedian Flula Borg.

Nicole Scherzinger Posts Comic Workout Session with Flula Borg

In an Instagram post from July 26, 2023, Scherzinger posted a short clip of her flexing her butt and jokingly making fun of Borg’s. In the video, both artists were wearing their striped workout leggings while doing a single dumbbell workout.

“Peaches and apricots,” she wrote.

Borg was also wearing a bralette top, slightly mimicking Scherzinger’s spaghetti-cut bralette. Ahead of the said Instagram post, the Pussycat Dolls vocalist uploaded a slideshow of their photos and comedic videos together.

It’s no secret that Scherzinger is keen in maintaining her sculpted physique. On her social media, the singer is generous in showing her routines, whether she’s alone or with her fiance Thom Evans.

She Gives Empowering Advice to Her Fans

Last July 24, the “Don’t Cha” hitmaker took the time to share her spiritual reflection. Posting a video of her while walking at the beach, Scherzinger gave advice through a voiceover.

“Don’t ever sink for anybody. You stand straight, you put your head and face forward, and you know exactly what you bring to the table,” the video says.

Fans then quickly expressed their agreement with the singer’s empowering tip in the comment section. Subsequently, they praised Scherzinger for staying true to her advice.

“Especially in an industry where it’s easy to change who you are or lose yourself, you’ve always stood tall and stayed true to yourself. I’ve always loved and admired that about you. Love you!” one user stated.

Amid the calm, there have been rumors speculating that Scherzinger has banned her Pussycat Dolls bandmates from attending her and Evans’ nuptials. An unknown source reported that the singer doesn’t want to bring any “toxic energy” to her wedding.
