University of South Bohemia in esk Budjovice

Additional information

We are proud to be a top-quality science & research centre specialising in education and research in natural, humanist and social sciences. It surely comes as no surprise since we are located in the wonderful region of South Bohemia. With its beautiful mountain ranges, national parks, winding rivers, countless ponds and other amazing natural sites, the region never stops inspiring us and it may just inspire you as well.

Since the campus is also not far from the city centre, you can easily enjoy the historical atmosphere of the town and its nightlife in addition to all the outdoor activities like sports or hiking. Considering that we are also located close to the borders of Germany and Austria, the list of activities is truly endless.

At USB you will benefit from a modern and dynamic environment and also from the individual approach to students that our staff of well-known scientists and professors considers essential.

USB is not only about interesting programmes and a wide range of courses in foreign languages. The University fosters close cooperation with 400 universities around the world, supports foreign studies and research trips by students and academic staff and offers a lot of exchange opportunities.

The University offers many highly reputable disciplines and prides itself on being a leading centre of science and research. You will meet top professionals and benefit from working with the Czech Academy of Sciences and other institutions. The vast majority of our graduates goes on to find employment in their chosen field.

In addition, we are also proud to have a fantastic campus full of life and greenery. The school, dormitories and the library are all located close together and you can look forward to many concerts and events that are regularly held on campus. We are always working on improving the student experience and the upgraded facilities also include a refectory with good food for subsidized prices as well as a kindergarten, student club, coffee shop, Wi-Fi and a cash machine (ATM).

The University of South Bohemia in České Budějovice is simply unique, smart and beautiful, but it is also embedded in a fascinating town and region offering scenic countryside, cultural & historical sites as well as places to enjoy sports.

Come find your future at USB!
