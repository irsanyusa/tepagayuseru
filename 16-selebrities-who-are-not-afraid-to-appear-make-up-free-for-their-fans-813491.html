16 elebrities Who Are Not Afraid to Appear Make-Up Free for Their Fans

Can you believe that Charlie Chaplin once got third place in a contest where people dressed up as Charlie Chaplin? That’s the power of makeup! It can completely change a person’s appearance and give them a whole new identity. Sometimes, that new identity becomes so influential that even most celebrities find it hard to let go. However, there are a few instances where stars choose to embrace their natural beauty and show us a glimpse of their less glamorous side.

1. Jennifer Lopez

Jennifer Lopez, also known as J.Lo, is a multi-talented woman. She acts, sings, dances, and designs fashion. The photo shows her without makeup before a stage performance. It’s difficult to determine who looks more beautiful: J.Lo with or without makeup.

2. Britney Spears

The “Princess of Pop” is considered a pop icon and is credited with reviving teen pop. While we seldom see her without makeup, she prefers to stay without makeup at home. Still pretty glamorous, isn’t she?

3. Adele

Adele looks stunning in her looks. However, she confidently embraces going makeup-free in public. Her Instagram feed is filled with spontaneous no-cosmetic selfies that emerge whenever she feels like it.

4. Gal Gadot

When she’s not saving the world as Wonder Woman, Gal Gadot prefers to spend time with her 2 daughters. Though unrecognizable compared to her Wonder Woman persona, Gal still looks stunning in this photograph while watching The Cat in the Hat with her daughter.

5. Cindy Crawford

She looks GREAT with or without!

As one of the most famous supermodels of the 1980s and 1990s, Cindy Crawford has been featured on the covers of the most prestigious magazines, including Elle, People, and Vogue, over 500 times. It is, therefore, almost impossible to imagine that she ever gets to remove her makeup. Luckily for us, she shared her post-spa photograph.

6. Carrie Underwood

WOW. This one NEEDS makeup!

The winner of 3 Grammys, Carrie Underwood is a unique combination of beauty and talent. Although unrecognizable due to the absence of makeup, she looks gorgeous in this photograph while sweating it out at the gym.

7. Lady Gaga

Lady Gaga is a makeup chameleon. People’s opinions of her style may vary, but we all adore her, even without cosmetics.

8. Drew Barrymore

Drew Barrymore likes to keep it as real as possible when she is not filming, and this is not the only instance of the camera capturing her without makeup. Without it, she is undoubtedly cuter than her sexier with-makeup persona.

9. Emilia Clarke

The mother of dragons, sans all the makeup we are used to seeing her in!!

10. Jessica Alba

Stunning beauty, for sure!

For someone who has been used to getting all glammed up since the age of 13 and also has her own beauty brand, it is surprising to see her without makeup. Yet she looks every bit as beautiful as always.

11. Tyra Banks

Tyra Banks has been ranked as one of the most influential people by Time, and not without reason. Her strength helped her decide to share a completely makeup-free selfie on Instagram. While she might think people will consider the selfie “rough,” we certainly believe otherwise.

12. Sharon Stone

A sex symbol in her own right, Sharon Stone manages to look equally stunning even without makeup, even though she’s a little unrecognizable.

13. Zoe Saldana

After following an extensive makeup routine for her role as Neytiri in the movie Avatar, it is not surprising to see her trying to avoid makeup. But we really want her to get back to her role for the sequel, Avatar 2.

14. Gwyneth Paltrow

While people tend to put on all sorts of makeup to look their most beautiful on their birthday, Gwyneth Paltrow chose to go the other way. In this photograph, shared on her 44th birthday, we must agree that she looks different but highly graceful.

15. Naomi Watts

When someone looks so beautiful even without makeup, it’s understandable that she is regularly featured on various “most beautiful people” lists. But, surely, if we were to meet her without makeup on the streets, it would be tough to recognize her.

16. Selena Gomez

Really? Is that Selena Gomez or the beautiful girl next door returning home after a fun night out?

17. Shakira

Another girl trying the makeup-free birthday look!! No matter how much or how little makeup she wears, Shakira still manages to look glamorous.
