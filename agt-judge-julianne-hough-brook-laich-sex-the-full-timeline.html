Julianne Hough is winning our hearts as a new AGT judge this season, and she’s also been making headlines quite often in recent months. Julianne and her husband, hockey player Brooks Laich, have been dropping bombs about their sex life, and it’s grabbing people’s attention. Is Julianne engaging in clever marketing or is it just coincidental honesty? You be the judge.

If there was any doubt left that sex sells, Julianne has settled the debate this year. We put together a timeline of her most titillating moments, from posing nude to … having her toes sucked?

1) Julianne Reveals Her Sex Struggles With Endometriosis

January, 2019: Julianne Hough opened up about her experience with endometriosis, including how it affects her sex life. The condition can cause pain during sex, which Julianne says she has experienced with Brooks. However, they’ve found ways to deal with it.

“It can definitely cut things short. Sometimes we’re in the middle and I’m just like ‘AH, stop!’” she told Women’s Health, adding, “There’s so much intimacy without actually having sex. There are some cool things we’ve learned and it’s literally been awesome.”

2) Julianne And Her Husband Are Trying IVF Treatment

June 2019: Julianne’s husband Brooks Laich, told Us Weekly that the couple is undergoing IVF (in vitro fertilization) in the hope of having a baby together.

“My wife and I want to have children in our future, and going through IVF was a decision we made to increase the [odds] of that happening,” Brooks said. “I wish people would perceive it [that way instead of with] shame or guilt.”

Brooks Laich’s podcast is all about issues such as this, which are “stereotyped or deemed sensitive or hush-hush”.

3) Julianne Stars In A Sexy Pride Month Video

June 2019: Julianne Hough appeared in a video with dancer and Instagram star Conor McKenzie to celebrate LGBTQ Pride Month. The pair showed off some fabulous dance moves while wearing a variety of sexy outfits.

“We wanted to do something extremely special to show our love and support for freedom of self expression,” Julianne wrote on Facebook, “and dance is how we both feel totally and completely self expressed.”

4) Brooks And Julianne Reveal Their Sex Secrets

July 2019: Brooks hosts a podcast called How Men Think, and Julianne appeared as a guest last month. The two got honest about their sex life, with Julianne saying she looked into an online sex therapist to learn their “erotic blueprint.”

“By almost learning each other’s language… it was almost like a menu of how we could please each other sexually, but also intimately,” she said. Meanwhile, Brooks shared that he loves when Julianne wears lingerie to bed, saying it gets him “f**king fired up.”

5) Julianne Poses Nude On Women’s Health

August 2019: Julianne stripped down for the September 2019 “Naked Strength” issue of the magazine. “I didn’t want to do a demure shoot where I was trying to cover my body,” she said. “I wanted to do something where I was free.”

In an Instagram video, she added that posing nude was a “metaphor,” adding, “It’s more about what this journey was for me this year, about shedding and de-layering and coming back to the core and the essence of who I am and how I got there.”

6) Julianne Reveals She’s “Not Straight”

In the same Women’s Health issue, Julianne also opened up about her sexuality, revealing that she’s “not straight,” and sharing how she told Brooks.

“I [told him], ‘You know I’m not straight, right?’ And he was like, ‘I’m sorry, what?’ I was like, ‘I’m not. But I choose to be with you,’” she said, adding that now “there’s no fear of voicing things that I’ve been afraid to admit or that I’ve had shame or guilt about because of what I’ve been told or how I was raised.”

August 2019: Brooks revealed on his podcast that Julianne enjoys some interesting things in the bedroom. “My wife really enjoys it when I suck on her toes. True story. Hey, we play around. Why not?” he said.

However, Brooks doesn’t ask her to reciprocate, since his feet are in “skates and workout shoes” so often. “I can shower and soap them up, but I would never,” he said.

TMI? Maybe. But it definitely has people talking.

All of these revelations are contributing to the recent rise of Julianne Hough as a household celebrity name.
