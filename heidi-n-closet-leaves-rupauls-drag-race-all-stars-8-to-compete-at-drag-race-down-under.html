RuPaul’s Drag Race All Stars 8 has been nothing short of a disaster, leaving fans and queens disheartened by the unfolding chaos. Amidst the turmoil, beloved queen Heidi N Closet has made a shocking decision to leave the competition on her own terms. However, she has also given fans something to look forward to?? Is Heidi N Closet going to be in the upcoming season of Drag Race Down Under? Let’s investigate.

Heidi N Closet Leaves Drag Race Following A Dramatic Season

In a surprising turn of events, Heidi N Closet chose to self-eliminate during a heated argument in the Werk Room. This rare occurrence in Drag Race history has left fans and fellow contestants stunned.

Heidi’s decision to leave the show, sacrificing the time and money invested in preparing for the season, has left a somber atmosphere and raised concerns about the well-being of the contestants. Her departure only further intensified the negative atmosphere that has plagued All Stars 8.

Fans have been vocal about their dissatisfaction with the judging this season, with accusations of favoritism being hurled at the show’s producers. The heavy-handed nature of the judging panel has frustrated many viewers, who allege that they have overlooked certain queens while providing preferential treatment to others.

Even queens like Heidi N Closet remained in the safe zone until her departure, leaving fans questioning the fairness of the competition. Additionally, Mrs. Kasha Davis’ stellar performances in the acting challenges went unrecognized, further fueling the discontent among fans.

Heidi leaving the show had a ripple effect on the other contestants, with Kahanna Montrese considering following suit. The gravity of the situation prompted RuPaul to personally intervene and enter the Werk Room to persuade the queens to stay. This unprecedented action by RuPaul, reminiscent of Adore’s departure in All Stars 2, showcased the fragility of the season and the potential for it to completely unravel.

Heidi N Closet Jokes She’ll Be In Drag Race Down Under

In the midst of all the chaos and drama, Heidi N Closet’s joke of her appearance on the next season of Drag Race Down Under is a ray of hope into the hearts of fans. Despite her departure from All Stars 8, Heidi’s exciting future endeavors provide a glimmer of anticipation and a chance for redemption. To clarify, Heidi N Closet made a joke on Twitter, due to her All Stars 8 snatch game outfit. She didn’t actually announce her involvement in the series. Instead, this tweet was a meme.

Season Three of RuPaul’s Drag Race Down Under is set to premiere on July 28, and fans are eagerly anticipating another exciting and fierce competition. Hosted by RuPaul, the season will feature returning judges Michelle Visage and Rhys Nicholson, ensuring a captivating and entertaining experience for viewers.

The highly anticipated season of RuPaul’s Drag Race Down Under will air on WOW Presents Plus in the United States and select territories worldwide. Fans in New Zealand can catch the local airing on TVNZ+, while viewers in Australia can tune in on Stan.
