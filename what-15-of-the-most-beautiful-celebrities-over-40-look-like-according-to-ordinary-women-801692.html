What 15 of the Most Beautiful Celebrities Over 40 Look Like, According to Ordinary Women

The aesthetic clinic Glowday conducted a survey to find out which celebrity over 40 was best at keeping her charm. As it turned out, not all of the famous women listed spend much time worrying about beauty procedures.

At Bright Side, we decided to show this list to our readers and reveal the secrets of these famous beauties at the same time.

15. Kate Beckinsale, 47 years old

Kate Beckinsale is an actress best known for movies like Pearl Harbor, Van Helsing, and the Underworld series. 8% of the people polled mentioned her. She uses nothing special to take care of her skin, just simple soap, sunscreen, and oils.

14. Claudia Winkleman, 49 years old

The English television presenter says that she doesn’t have any strict beauty routine. Sometimes she even goes to bed without removing her makeup. The only thing Winkleman recommends is to get more sleep.

13. Helen Mirren, 75 years old

Helen Mirren is still a sought-after actress who acts in many popular movies. But it’s worth noting that she doesn’t think of herself as beautiful. She is not inclined to share her beauty secrets, but if she happened to be in a drugstore with $20, she would spend it on lipstick, lip liner, and moisturizer.

12. Davina McCall, 53 years old

Davina McCall is an English actress and television presenter. She has a motivational self-care routine from morning to evening. She gets up as soon as the sun comes up, looks in the mirror often, exercises a lot, takes vitamins, drinks collagen tea, and eats only healthy food. In her free time, she likes to take baths with salts and a good book.

11. Kate Hudson, 41 years old

It’s no surprise that the actress happened to make it onto this list. She’s very thorough about her appearance and nutrition. She uses a lot of beauty products, visits beauticians, and doesn’t feel shy about wearing face masks, even while on an airplane. Hudson drinks a lot of water and meditates. She believes that the secret to beauty is being healthy.

10. Sarah Jessica Parker, 56 years old

The leading star of Sex and the City says that she works with professionals who make her hair look good while she doesn’t use any special beauty products — just soap, shampoo, and moisturizer in the morning and evening.

9. Nigella Lawson, 61 years old

Nigella Lawson is a British journalist, food writer, and television cook. She shared one of her beauty life hacks: she uses washing-up gloves to exfoliate and keep her skin in great condition.

8. Reese Witherspoon, 45 years old

The leading star of Big Little Lies and Oscar-winner, Reese Witherspoon, looks really young for her age. She combines strength training with high-intensity cardio, eats plenty of greens, doesn’t skimp on sunscreen, takes water with her everywhere she goes, and sets aside time for self-care.

7. Kate Moss, 47 years old

Kate is a supermodel who was selected by 15% of poll-takers. Her biggest skin secret is dunking her head in a bowl of ice cubes, cold water, and slices of cucumber and holding her face in for as long as she can. It instantly reduces puffiness and tightens up the skin.

6. Tess Daly, 51 years old

Tess Daly is a journalist and television presenter. She says that she cleanses, tones, and moisturizes her skin in the morning and evening. She also meditates, does yoga, and swims. She recommends jumping rope in the morning. It will help you wake up and get your circulation going.

5. Naomi Campbell, 50 years old

Thanks to 20% of the votes, there is another supermodel on the list. Naomi Campbell uses a facial roller, moisturizing sprays and creams, applies her makeup only before work, and always removes it before going to bed. According to Campbell, it’s all about staying hydrated and having radiant, shiny skin.

4. Victoria Beckham, 46 years old

The British singer and designer definitely deserves this high position. She swears by regular facial massages, uses light therapy and natural facial products, and is a big fan of eye masks. She also can’t live without different moisturizers.

3. Kim Kardashian, 40 years old

Kim Kardashian greatly influenced makeup artists all over the world because her main secret is great makeup. According to Kim, a spoonful of sugar helps your skin glow. She adds the sweet stuff to her body wash “for a gentle exfoliating experience.”

2. Jennifer Aniston, 52 years old

Jennifer Aniston needs no introduction thanks to her artistic talent and memorable appearance. And this is the result of not just her genes but also her strong will. She washes her face with ice water, swears by infrared sessions, exercises and meditates, prefers organic beauty products, and swaps out her coffee for celery juice. She almost never uses makeup and avoids excessive heat styling on her hair.

1. Jennifer Lopez, 51 years old

The actress and singer received 38% of the votes and became the undisputed leader, overtaking Aniston by 4%. At the same time, she doesn’t like to talk about her age and looks because she thinks that she’s youthful and timeless at every age. Her main tricks are sincerity, control over her emotions, and olive oil, which was her mother’s beauty secret.
