New for 2024, Breaking (or Breakdancing) makes its debut in the Summer Olympics in Paris. More than 30 B-Boys and B-Girls from over 15 nations are competing for the gold, including the United States, Japan, Morocco, The Netherlands and more.

Men’s and Women’s Breaking starts on Friday, Aug. 9 to Saturday, Aug. 10 with a start time of 10 a.m. ET/7 a.m. PT each day. The event takes place at Place de la Concorde in Paris, France.

Want to watch Breaking during the Summer Olympics in Paris online? Here’s what you need to know.

How To Watch Breaking: Summer Olympics 2024 Online

NBCUniversal acquired all of the broadcast and streaming rights for the 2024 Summer Olympic Games, including Breaking. All battles are available with a subscription to Peacock, which starts at $7.99/Month for the ad-supported plan, or $79.99/Year when you sign up for an annual subscription here (that’s 12 months for the price of 10).
