Seyi Law Rejoices as He Bags Political Appointment, Fans React: &quot;You Could Not Speak the Truth&quot;

  • Comedian Seyi Law has been having some political fights in the past months and getting tackled for supporting the APC government
  • According to some people, his support for the government has finally paid off, as he was rewarded with a political appointment
  • As he shared the good news online, he got mixed reactions from netizens who lambasted him for not staying on the side of the truth

Nigerian comedian, Oluwaseyitan Lawrence Aletile, aka Seyi Law, has expressed excitement after the Ondo state governor, Lucky Aiyedatiwa, made him a senior special assistant on entertainment and tourism.

He posted the good news on his Instagram page and thanked the governor for the appointment. The funnyman also prayed that God would help them fulfil the state's vision.

His post generated mixed reactions from netizens, who lambasted him for not joining the people to fight the All Progressive Congress-led government.
