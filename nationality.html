Famous Kenyans in History - On This Day

List of Kenyan People

  • 1902-10-26 Beryl Markham (née Clutterbuck), British-Kenyan; racehorse trainer, aviator, and memoirist, born in Ashwell, Rutland, England (d. 1986)
  • 1920-10-31 Dedan Kimathi [wa Waciuri], Kenyan freedom fighter (Mau Mau Uprising), born in Thegenge Village, British Kenya (d. 1957)
  • 1931-11-15 Mwai Kibaki, Kenyan politician, President of Kenya (2002-13), born in Gatuyaini, Kenya (d. 2022)
  • 1936-03-22 Roger Whittaker, Kenyan-British country singer ("The Last Farewell"; "Durham Town"), born in Nairobi, Kenya (d. 2023)
  • 1938-01-05 Ngũgĩ wa Thiong'o, Kenyan writer, born in Kamirithu, British Kenya
  • 1940-01-17 Kipchoge "Kip" Keino, Kenyan athlete (Olympic gold 1,500m 1968, 3k steeplechase 1972), born in Kipsamo, Kenya
  • 1940-04-01 Wangari Maathai, Kenyan environmentalist and political activist, founder of the Green Belt Movement, recipient of the Nobel Peace Prize (2004) and Indira Gandhi Peace Prize (2006), born in Ihithe village, Kenya (d. 2011)
  • 1942-02-12 Chananjit Vohra, Kenyan-British hotel magnate
  • 1943-08-29 Mohamed Amin, Kenyan photojournalist, born in Eastleigh, Nairobi, Kenya (d. 1996)
  • 1944-12-19 Richard Leakey, Kenyan paleoanthropologist, conservationist and politician, born in Nairobi, Kenya
  • 1945-01-07 Raila Odinga, Kenyan politician, Prime Minister of Kenya (2008-2013), born in Maseno, Kenya
  • 1945-10-09 Naftali Bon, Kenyan 4x400m runner (Olympic silver 1968), born in Kapsabet, Rift Valley Province, Kenya
  • 1947-09-08 Amos Biwott, Kenyan 3K steeplechaser (Olympic gold 1968), born in Uasin Gishee, Kenya
  • 1949-01-06 Mike Boit, Kenyan 800m runner (Olympic bronze 1972), born in Eldoret, Kenya
  • 1954-10-24 Wahome Muthahi, Kenyan humorist, newspaper columnist (The Daily Nation - Whispers), and playwright, born in Kenya (d. 2003)
  • 1955-05-29 Sut Jhally, Kenyan-born American educator
  • 1958-02-18 Peter Koech, Kenyan 3K steeplechaser (Olympic silver 1988), born in Kiliburani, Kenya
  • 1958-06-03 Ibrahim Hussein, Kenyan marathon runner (Boston Marathon-1988, 91, 92), born in Kapsabet, Kenya
  • 1961-02-21 Yobes Ondieki, Kenyan 10K runner (world record)
  • 1961-04-12 Julius Kariuki, Kenyan 3K steeplechaser (Olympic gold 1988), born in Nyahururu, Kenya
  • 1961-10-26 Uhuru Kenyatta, Kenyan Prime Minister (2013-2022) and son of Jomo Kenyatta, born in Nairobi, Kenya
  • 1962-07-10 Charles Papa, Kenyan comedian and actor (Papa Shirandula), born in Busia, Kenya (d. 2020)
  • 1963-09-26 Douglas Wakiihuri, Kenyan athlete (World marathon champion 1987, Olympic silver 1988), born in Mombasa, Kenya
  • 1965-05-06 Zahid Sadiq, Kenyan cricketer, born in Nairobi, Kenya
  • 1966-02-25 Samson Kitur, Kenyan athlete (d. 2003)
  • 1966-06-25 Paula Kahumbu, Kenyan-born wildlife activist (Hands Off Our Elephants campaign), born in Nairobi, Kenya
  • 1966-12-21 William Ruto, Kenyan politician (President of Kenya 2022-, Deputy President 2013-2022), born in Kamagut, Kenya
  • 1968-08-22 Paul Ereng, Kenyan athlete, 800m runner (Olympic gold 1988), born in Kitale, Kenya
  • 1969-06-17 Paul Tergat, Kenyan long distance runner, born in Riwo, Baringo District, Kenya
  • 1970-08-23 Joseph Chebet, Kenyan athlete (Boston Marathon 1999, New York Marathon 1999, Amsterdam Marathon 1998, Vienna Marathon 2003), born in Eldoret, Kenya (d. 2023)
  • 1970-09-18 Dan Eldon, British-Kenyan photojournalist, born in Hampstead, London (d. 1993)
  • 1970-10-01 Moses Kiptanui, Kenyan steeplechaser (World C'ship gold 1991, 93, 95; Olympic silver 1996), born in Marakwet District, Kenya
  • 1971-01-18 Binyavanga Wainaina, Kenyan author and journalist, born in Nakuru, Rift Valley
  • 1971-06-25 Steve Tikolo, Kenyan cricketer (outstanding Kenyan batsman in 1996 World Cup), born in Nairobi, Kenya
  • 1972-12-12 Wilson Kipketer, Kenyan-born Danish middle-distance runner, born in Kapchemoiywo, Nandi County, Kenya
  • 1974-02-11 Zain Verjee, Kenyan-born journalist, born in Nairobi, Kenya
  • 1974-08-23 Benjamin Limo, Kenyan runner, born in Eldoret, Kenya
  • 1978-04-22 Paul Malakwen Kosgei, Kenyan long-distance and marathon runner, born in Marakwet, Kenya
  • 1978-09-26 Robert Kipkoech Cheruiyot, Kenyan long-distance runner, born in Kapsabet, Kenya
  • 1979-03-10 Edi Gathegi, Kenyan actor (Blacklist), born in Nairobi, Kenya
  • 1979-08-02 Reuben Kosgei, Kenyan athlete, born in Kapsabet, Kenya
  • 1980-07-24 Wilfred Bungei, Kenyan middle-distance runner
  • 1980-08-29 Mohammad Sheikh, Kenyan cricketer, born in Nairobi, Kenya
  • 1981-07-27 Collins Obuya, Kenyan cricket all-rounder (104 ODIs, 35 wickets, 11 x 50), born in Nairobi, Kenya
  • 1983-03-01 Lupita Nyong’o, Mexican-Kenyan actress (12 Years a Slave), born in Mexico City
  • 1984-11-05 Eliud Kipchoge, Kenyan distance runner (Olympic gold marathon 2016; first under 2:00), born in Kapsisiywa, Kenya
  • 1994-01-10 Faith Kipyegon, Kenyan athlete (Olympic gold women's 1,500m 2016, 20; World C'ship gold 2017), born in Bomet, Kenya
  • 1994-02-20 Brigid Kosgei, Kenyan athlete (marathon world record 2019; Chicago Marathon 2018-9, London Marathon 2019), born in Kenya
  • 2000-03-05 Beatrice Chebet, Kenyan long-distance runner (2024 Olympic Gold 5,000m, 10,000m world record holder), born in Kenya
  • 2001-06-29 Elsa Majimbo, Kenyan internet comedienne, born in Nairobi, Kenya
