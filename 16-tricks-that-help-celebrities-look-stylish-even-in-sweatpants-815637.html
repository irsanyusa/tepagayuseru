16 Tricks That Help Celebrities Look Stylish Even in Sweatpants

It happens that controversial clothing items become trendy: cycling pants, lingerie-style dresses, or even sweatpants. But even though these clothes are comfortable, they may look really weird sometimes. However, celebrities can look great in absolutely any clothes, with the help of some tricks though.

Bella Hadid proves that you can look elegant even in cycling pants. Just pair them with an oversized jacket and elegant pumps.

LaurentVu/SIPA/EAST NEWS

A lingerie-style dress is timeless. And in order not to look vulgar in it, you can, like Victoria Beckham, combine it with a stylish trench coat and complete the look with high heels.

TS, PacificCoastNews/EAST NEWS

Or you can balance out the lightweight dress with a strict black blazer, just as Eva Longoria did.

Cyril Pecquenard/KCS/East News

Emma Stone’s choice of accessories not only made her look complete, they also balanced the proportions: the chain elongated her neck and the belt emphasized her waistline.

Evan Agostini/Invision/East News

Jennifer Lawrence found a great way to balance the bold length of her skirt with a strict jacket and a see-through turtleneck. The outfit became more elegant, but no less sexy.

IMAGO/Ralf MŸller/Imago Stock and People/East News

Jennifer Lopez presented her tracksuit with glamour, complementing it with spectacular accessories: hoop earrings, a leather bag and large eyeglasses.

mcla@broadimage/Broad Image/East News

Sarah Jessica Parker made her skinny jeans look careless by rolling up one pant leg.

Evan Agostini/Invision/AP/East News

The same rule works with ordinary sweatpants. You just need to follow the celebrity’s example and pull up the pants just above your ankles.

Celine Dion knows that there should be only one bright accent in the entire outfit. Rhinestone boots will shine even brighter against the background of clothes of neutral colors.

Fishnet tights always attract attention, so it’s best to pair them with modest pieces, just like Gwyneth Paltrow did.

As you may know, culottes can make a person look bigger and shorter. Katie Holmes solved this problem with stiletto heels and long chains.

Victoria Beckham likes to wear light dresses even in the cold weather by pairing them with a turtleneck. It looks stylish, but most importantly it’s really warmer this way.

Starface/STARFACE PHOTO /East News

Beautiful Laetitia Casta knows how to make a men’s suit look sexy with the help of a lace bodysuit.

Sofía Vergara knows that a simple white men’s shirt will look more impressive if you roll up the sleeves.

TS, PacificCoastNews/EAST NEWS, Luis Guerra/The Grosby Group/EAST NEWS, thecelebrityfinder@broadimage.com/Broad Image/East News

Cate Blanchett suggests wearing sweatpants tucked into ultra-trendy ankle boots. Also pay attention that the actress is wearing a dress, not a blouse.

RB/Associated Press/East News

Thanks to the tights of a dark shade, the actress managed to balance her short dress with over-the-knee boots.
